Achievements

2019 Social Innovation Challenge

Top 5 Finalist

Last year, I participated in the 2019 Social Innovation Challenge pitch competition by World Vision. Based on the theme of waste management, the challenge was to pitch a product or service that would target the plastic waste problem in the Philippines city of Iloilo. Our team created LocalLife, a dual bulk food store and recycling-return center that fosters change through community action. In the end, our team was shortlisted and was one of the Top 5 Finalists out of 46 applicants.

Gut Microbiome Simulation


This project is part of the University Research Experience with Complex Systems (URECS) program at the University of Toronto. This program uses the software, COBWEB (Complex Organized Behaviour Within Environmental Boundaries) to model different types of complex systems in multiple areas of study. This software uses computer simulations to model certain behaviours or trends and can be used to display results of labs or test different theories. The model on the left is one of the gut microbiome and how it affects depression in the brain.
